Apple and Oat Bites




The "Apple and Oat Bites" is a recipe I proudly made myself! Therer are 5 simple ingredients, which are apple, maple syrup, pumpkin seeds, oats, and powdered whole wheat cereal. I really love the simplicity of this recipe, and how the apple chunks in the ball add that juciness, which is perfect for fall when apples are nice and fresh! I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as I did! ~Aishani U. :)




Ingredients:

    1 teaspoons powdered whole wheat cereal

    4 teaspoons oats

    1.5 teaspoons maple syrup

    finely cut apple pieces

    pumpkin seeds




Instructions:

    1) In a bowl, add the whole wheat cereal powder, oats, and apple

    2) Mix throughly and make sure the apple is combined into the powder

    3) Slowly add the maple syrup, little by little, into the mixture, an squish it thoroughly until it becomes doughy

    4) Add in the pumpkin seeds and thoroughly mix again

    5) Freeze the dough for about half an hour

    6) After taking out the freezer, roll into small balls

    7) Enjoy!
